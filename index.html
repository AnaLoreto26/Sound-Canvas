SOUND CANVAS

Have you ever wondered what our breath—or even the "silence"—looks like?
Have you ever seen your own voice?

Sound Canvas transforms sound frequencies into visual forms by combining variable parameters with volume, pitch, and rhythm.

This experimental project sparks curiosity and invites us to perceive sound in different ways, allowing us to see what we hear—or sometimes what we don’t.

It encourages us to explore the complexity of our voices, instruments, music, or any audible object around us.


Ana Loreto, 2025
